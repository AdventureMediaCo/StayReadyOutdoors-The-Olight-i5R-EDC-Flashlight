Why the Olight i5R is the Perfect EDC Flashlight

When it comes to everyday carry (EDC) gear, having a reliable flashlight is essential. Enter the Olight i5R—a heavy hitter in a compact package that everyone should consider adding to their gear. Slightly larger than a tube of chapstick, this little powerhouse is designed for those unexpected moments when you need a bright light.

Compact Yet Powerful

The Olight i5R packs a punch with a maximum output of 350 lumens and a throw distance of 64 meters. This makes it perfect for a variety of tasks, whether you’re camping in the great outdoors or just rummaging through your bag in low-light conditions. The two lighting modes—15 lumens for low-light tasks and a high-performance 350 lumens—ensure you have just the right amount of brightness without blinding yourself or others.

Rechargeable Convenience

Equipped with a customized 14500 lithium-ion rechargeable battery, the i5R makes charging easy with its USB-C compatibility. But that’s not all—it also works with standard AA batteries, offering versatility and peace of mind with widely available power sources. Whether you're on a camping trip or just out and about, you'll always have the power you need.

Built for Life's Adventures

Weighing in at just 2.12 oz and measuring 95.4 mm (3.76 inches), the i5R is compact enough to fit in your pocket, purse, or backpack without adding bulk. Its durable aluminum alloy body, combined with an IPX8 waterproof rating, ensures that it can withstand harsh conditions and survive immersion in water. This means you can take it on all your outdoor adventures without worrying about the weather.

Effortless Operation

Using the i5R is a breeze, thanks to its non-slip tactical tail switch. You can quickly toggle between modes, and with the momentary on functionality, you can keep it lit as long as you press the switch. This makes it not only practical but also incredibly user-friendly.

Lifetime Warranty

Olight stands behind the i5R with a lifetime warranty, giving you confidence in your purchase. Should any manufacturing defects or malfunctions arise, you’re covered, ensuring that this flashlight is a long-term investment in your safety and convenience.
